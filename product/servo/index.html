Servo-Controlled Voltage Stabilizer

Product Information

Servo Control Voltage Stabilizers are highly efficient Voltage Regulators that work equally well on any kind of load. It consists of a detector, a switching unit; a motorized Auto Transformer and Cut Off Circuit. In case of any deviation for the set voltage; the detector sends an error signal to the Control Circuit that in turn drives a motor to correct the voltage using winding on Auto Transformer.
